About the Game

Mafia II is an immersive action-adventure game developed by 2K Czech. Set in the 1940s and 1950s in the fictional city of Empire Bay, the game tells the story of Vito Scaletta, a young man entangled in the world of organized crime. With a compelling narrative, cinematic presentation, and an authentic depiction of post-war America, Mafia II offers a rich and engaging experience for players.

Mafia II is praised for its gripping storyline, period-accurate music, and dynamic world.
